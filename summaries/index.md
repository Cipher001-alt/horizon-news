These summaries are automatically generated by the [Horizon](https://github.com/loulianzhang/horizon-news) workflow, which:

- Aggregates content from multiple sources (Hacker News, Reddit, RSS, Telegram, GitHub, etc.)
- Scores items using AI
- Filters and enriches the most important stories
- Generates bilingual summaries in English and Chinese

Each summary is named `horizon-{date}-{language}.md`
